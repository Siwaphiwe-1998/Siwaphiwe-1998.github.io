    ABOUT US

    What we offer to the public

    We as the Municipality take responsibility to provide the public with a cleaner environment and keep the areas they live in and surroundings clean by providing proper support.

    Our goals

    In pursuit of the vision, we will create a democratic, responsive, effective and efficient municipality that will be able to deliver affordable quality services.

    Main Vision

    A developmental municipality that is self-sustainable to empower its citizens socially, economically and legislatively to ensure sustainable livelihood.
